WHO IS CHATGPT?

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI assistant made by OpenAI. It can answer questions, write and improve code, explain topics, brainstorm ideas, and help with everyday tasks in a conversational way.

It is not a human. It generates responses by learning patterns from large amounts of text and then predicting the next best words in a reply.
